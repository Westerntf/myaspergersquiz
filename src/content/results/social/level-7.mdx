## Social – Level 7

At this level, social interaction often feels like a foreign language. You may find yourself frequently misunderstood or isolated despite best efforts to connect.

**Common experiences:**
- High anxiety in even familiar social settings
- Inability to intuit others’ thoughts or feelings
- Preference for solitary activities

**Suggested supports:**
- Neurodivergent-affirming therapy
- Role-play or assisted interaction planning
- Online communities with clear structure and rules
