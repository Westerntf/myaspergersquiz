## Focus – Level 1

You have a very high capacity for focus and attention. You’re able to switch tasks or sustain effort with ease, even in distracting environments.

**Common experiences:**
- Completing tasks without needing breaks
- Adapting easily between topics or contexts
- Staying mentally present in conversations or routines

**Suggested supports:**
- Leverage your focus for deep work or leadership roles
- Help others stay on task in group settings
- Explore long-term goals that benefit from sustained effort
